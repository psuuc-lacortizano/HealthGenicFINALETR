Classes

FITNESS CLASSES


Cardio

Cardio is defined as any type of exercise that gets your heart rate up and keeps it up for a prolonged period of time.


Personal Training

A personal trainer is an individual who creates and delivers safe and effective exercise programs for healthy individuals and groups, or those with medical clearance to exercise.

Calisthenics

Calisthenics or callisthenics is a form of strength training consisting of a variety of movements that exercise large muscle groups, such as standing, grasping, pushing, etc.


DIET CLASSES


High-protein diet

A high-protein diet is a diet in which 20% or more of the total daily calories comes from protein.


Ketogenic diet

The ketogenic diet is a high-fat, adequate-protein, low-carbohydrate dietary therapy that in conventional medicine is used mainly to treat hard-to-control epilepsy in children.

Intermittent fasting

Intermittent fasting is any of various meal timing schedules that cycle between voluntary fasting and non-fasting over a given period.
